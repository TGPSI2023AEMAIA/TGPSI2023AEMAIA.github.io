Renewable energy sources

There are five major renewable energy sources

The major types or sources of renewable energy are:

  1. Solar energy from the sun
  2. Geothermal energy from heat inside the earth
  3. Wind energy
  4. Biomass from plants
  5. Hydropower from flowing water

Renewable energy meaning

Renewable energy is energy that is generated from natural processes that are continuosly replenished. This inclues sunlight, geothermal heat, wind, tides, water, and various forms of biomass. This energy cannot be exhausted and is constantly renewed.

Solar energy

Solar energy is an essential source of renewable energy and is harnessed using a wide range of technologies such as solar power to generate electricity. And the large magnitude of solar energy available makes it an appealing soruce of eletricity, and is normally cheaper than fossil fuels since 2021.

Energetic efficiency

Energetic efficiency meaning

Energy efficiency simply means using less energy to perform the same task - that is, eleminating energy waste.

Benefits of energy efficiency

Energy efficiency brings a variety of benefits: reducing greenhouse gas emissions, reducing demand for energy imports, and lowering our costs on a hosehold and economy-wide level.

While renewable energy technologies also help accomplish these objectives, improving energy efficiency is the cheapest - and often the most immediate - way to reduce the use of fossil fuels

The efficiency needs to improve

There are anourmous opportunities for efficiency improvements in every sector of the economy, whether it ist buildings, tranportation, industry, or energy generation.

Solar cells

Solar cells are any device that directly converts the energy of the light into electrical energy trough the photovoltaic effect. Solar cells can be arranged into large groupings called arrays. These arrays, composed of many thousands of individual cells, can function as central eletric power stations, converting sunlight into electrical energy for distribution to industrial, commericial, and residential users.
The use of solar cells could help to achieve a sustainable energy future.
